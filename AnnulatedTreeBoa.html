Annulated Tree Boa

Corralus annulatus

Annulated or Ringed Tree boas are another smaller slender species of tree boa reaching 4-6ft on average. They have two natural occurring color forms one with redish orange base color the other silver grey base color. Both color forms have a ring like pattern with darker brown and black rings in a net like pattern. Some individuals get white blotches on the flanks. Predominantly found in Central America from south eastern Guatemala through to western Ecuador in South America.

I work with a small group of this fascinating boa.
