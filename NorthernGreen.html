Northern Green Python

Morelia a. utaraensis

The Northern Green Python is a small python averaging around 3 to 4ft. As the common name suggests the Northern Green python inhabits areas with suitable habitat on the island of New Guinea north of the central dividing range. Trade names often associated with this species are Cylopse, Jayapura, Lerah, Bokondini, Wamena, and Yapen.

The Northern Species has both Red and Yellow Babies. Adults are typically a deep green adorned with blue spinal pattern of varying intensity along with variable amounts of white or yellow speckling mostly found on either side the spine withing pattern elements. The tail of this taxa is pointed and yellow or green.

I work with a small group of founder adults I acquired as Cylopse specimens. I have produced F1 offspring from my adults.
