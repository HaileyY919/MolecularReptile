Diamond Python

Morelia s. spilota

Diamond Pythons are medium sized pythons averaging 5-7ft. They occur on the South eastern coast of Australia from eastern New South Wales to eastern Victoria. They are a stunning species predominantly black with white or yellow speckles with small blotches or bands of pattern.

I work with a pair and have yet to breed this taxa.
