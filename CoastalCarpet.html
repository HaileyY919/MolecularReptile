Southern Coastal Carpet Python

Morelia s. mcdowelli

Southern Coastal Carpet pythons are the largest form of carpet python. They average 5-8ft but much larger specimens have been reported. They occur on the east coast of Australia from central Queensland to Northern New South Wales. They are colored with shades of brown and straw with banded and striped specimens. Babies can be brown to red.

I work for a pair originating from near Brisbane but I have yet to breed this taxa.
