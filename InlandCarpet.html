Inland Carpet Python

Morelia s. metcalfei

Inland Carpet pythons are an average sized python ranging from 4 to 7ft. They occur from south east South Australia, northern Victoria up into central Queensland. They are colored with shades of grey and brown with black banded patterning and soft reddish orange highlights.

I work with a pair of this taxa.
